#pat A1109 Group Photo

1109 Group Photo (25分)

题目来源：https://pintia.cn/problem-sets/994805342720868352/problems/994805360043343872

封面：Photo by C MA on Unsplash

Formation is very important when taking a group photo. Given the rules of forming K rows with N people as the following:

  • The number of people in each row must be N/K (round down to the nearest integer), with all the extra people (if any) standing in the last row;
  • All the people in the rear row must be no shorter than anyone standing in the front rows;
  • In each row, the tallest one stands at the central position (which is defined to be the position (m/2+1), where m is the total number of people in that row, and the division result must be rounded down to the nearest integer);
  • In each row, other people must enter the row in non-increasing order of their heights, alternately taking their positions first to the right and then to the left of the tallest one (For example, given five people with their heights 190, 188, 186, 175, and 170, the final formation would be 175, 188, 190, 186, and 170. Here we assume that you are facing the group so your left-hand side is the right-hand side of the one at the central position.);
  • When there are many people having the same height, they must be ordered in alphabetical (increasing) order of their names, and it is guaranteed that there is no duplication of names.

Now given the information of a group of people, you are supposed to write a program to output their formation.

Input Specification:

Each input file contains one test case. For each test case, the first line contains two positive integers N (≤104), the total number of people, and K (≤10), the total number of rows. Then N lines follow, each gives the name of a person (no more than 8 English letters without space) and his/her height (an integer in [30, 300]).

Output Specification:

For each case, print the formation – that is, print the names of people in K lines. The names must be separated by exactly one space, but there must be no extra space at the end of each line. Note: since you are facing the group, people in the rear rows must be printed above the people in the front rows.

要求按照规则来模拟拍照的排列，本质上是个排序题，sort就完事了，但是输出比较麻烦，要求很多。一开始竟然还在思考什么样的数据结构可以让我左边插入一个右边插入一个，后面觉得插入什么啊直接输出就完事了。输出研究得比较久，数组下标没有打草稿我真的有点懵哈哈哈～

输出大概的做法是：因为所有人已经按照身高降序排列了，按照题意把人分成K行，K个区间，start记录每个区间起始的下标，end记录每个区间末位的下标，但是左闭右开，也就是区间下标是[start,end)这个范围内的。pos记录的下标用来输出。

先输出左边。对于每个区间，如果是偶数个人，那输出的第一个人就是区间最后一个人，下标end-1，如果是奇数个人，那么输出区间倒数第二个人，下标end-2。左边区间的规律是，前一个人是后面一个人的下标减2。

输出完左边，输出中间。中间就是这个区间最高的那个人，按照降序排列的list，输出的就是区间的start下标所指的那个人。

最后输出右边。右边就比较方便，直接从中间输出的最高的人的下标开始往后加就可以了，因为（大约）一半人去了左边，所以后一个人下标是前一个人下标加2.

下标很容易弄错，debug了好久。总之还是比较麻烦的一个题，考试可能会很烦吧哈哈哈～
